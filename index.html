Scenic Hudson's Sea Level Rise online mapper

The Sea Level Rise Mapper is a tool for communities and stakeholders to create visualizations of future scenarios of sea level rise.

Scenic Hudson's Sea Level Rise data has been updated by James Mudd, Conservation GIS Manager, Scenic Hudson, Inc.

  • See the online mapper: Scenic Hudson's SLR Mapper.
  • All elevation information is from a high-resolution LiDAR topography product collected by the NYS DEC Hudson River Estuary Program in 2012.
